About

We are a a group of Minecraft players who play together on a vanilla survival server! In the time we have been playing together, we have built a community of friends who enjoy playing together and building amazing things. We are always looking for new friends to join us; if you are interested, reach us on

Discord!

Meet the Founders.

Tarp and Palm, who were near strangers at the time, created LichenTown together on March 26th, 2024, with the goal of creating a community of friends who enjoy playing Minecraft together. They have been playing Minecraft together ever since, and have had many amazing adventures together, thanks to the people who have made LichenTown their home.

  • Discord: palm1 & ducky_what

Though Tarp and Palm planted the seed, it is the citizens of LichenTown who have made it what it is today. We thank each and every person who has contributed their time and creativity to make LichenTown special.
